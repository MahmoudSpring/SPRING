SPRING SARL

SPRING S.A.R.L is a Lebanese company established in 2007 as a trader for herbicides and pesticides. With a well-trained personnel and agriculture engineers, Spring SARL acquired a good working knowledge in the Lebanese area, and a very large chain of business relationship with several worldwide wholesaler and local Key farmers.
